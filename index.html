Alex Lamson

Hi, I'm a second year Computer Science Master's student at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. I do research in the MOSAIC lab at UMass, where I use machine learning to solve problems related to health and sensing. I'll be graduating with my Master's degree in May 2020, and I'm actively searching for a job.

In my spare time I like to read manga, rollerblade, and watch movies. I'm also interested in quantified self, and do self-tracking experiments as a hobby.
